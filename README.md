# Capture what!!! (Godot Wild Jam #94)

Drag turtles, dodge sharks, and avoid deadly jellyfish as you build your ultimate underwater catch.

![Game Jam Event](https://img.itch.zone/aW1hZ2UyL2phbS80MTM2OTkvMjUyNjgzMjAucG5n/original/j6AEIB.png)

# Controls
A and D to move left and right
Click and hold the Left Mouse Button to drag and capture turtles

# Gameplay
Capture the turtles by clicking and dragging them near your player. Try to catch as many as you can.
